Trabzon City, Turkey

About Trabzon

Trabzon, located on the Black Sea coast of northeastern Turkey, is a historic city with a rich cultural heritage. Known for its stunning natural beauty, delicious cuisine, and the iconic Hagia Sophia of Trabzon, it's a popular destination for tourists and history buffs alike.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Hagia Sophia of Trabzon
  • Explore the Atatürk Köşkü (Atatürk Pavilion)
  • Hike in the breathtaking Sümela Monastery
  • Relax on the beaches of the Black Sea
  • Try the local cuisine, including Akçaabat meatballs and kuymak
